<p><ul><li>Single Acting Air Hydraulic Pump</li><li>Double Acting Air Hydraulic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Hydraulic Pump market primarily consists of two types: single acting and double acting pumps. Single acting air hydraulic pumps use air pressure to drive the hydraulic fluid in one direction, ideal for applications requiring lifting or pressing only once per cycle. In contrast, double acting air hydraulic pumps offer more efficiency by utilizing air pressure to move fluid in both directions, enabling faster cycle times and greater versatility. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for lifting and material handling.</p></p>
