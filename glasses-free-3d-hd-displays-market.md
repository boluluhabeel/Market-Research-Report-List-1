<p><p>Glasses-free 3D HD displays utilize various technologies to create immersive viewing experiences without special eyewear. Light barrier technology uses an array of barriers to guide images to specific viewing angles. Lenticular lens technology employs lenses to project different images to each eye. Directional backlight involves illuminating the display from specific angles for 3D effects. Direct imaging technology utilizes a combination of optics and cameras to create a 3D effect, while "others" encompass emerging and hybrid techniques in the market.</p></p>
