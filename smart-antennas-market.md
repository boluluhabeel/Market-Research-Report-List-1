<p><strong>The Smart Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SIMO</li><li>MIMO</li><li>MISO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart antennas are advanced technologies that enhance wireless communication by improving signal quality and capacity. The market can be categorized into three types: SIMO (Single Input Multiple Output) uses one transmitter and multiple receivers to enhance signal reception; MISO (Multiple Input Single Output) utilizes multiple transmitters and a single receiver for improved transmission quality; while MIMO (Multiple Input Multiple Output) employs multiple transmitters and receivers, significantly increasing data rates and system capacity, making it ideal for modern wireless applications.</p></p>
