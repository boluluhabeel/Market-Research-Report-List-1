<p><p>LCD Brightness Enhancement Films (BEF) improve the luminance efficiency of displays by directing light more effectively. The market includes Normal Prism Sheets, which scatter light to enhance brightness; Multi-Functional Prism Sheets that combine multiple features for optimized performance; Micro-Lens Films that focus light into specific areas for improved clarity; and Reflective Polarizers (DBEF), which reflect unutilized light back into the display, maximizing brightness. Together, these films enhance visual experience in various LCD applications, driving growth in the display market.</p></p>
