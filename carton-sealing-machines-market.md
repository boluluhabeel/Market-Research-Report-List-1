<p><p>Carton sealing machines are essential for packaging operations, and they come in various types, including top head and bottom head machines. Top head machines apply adhesive or tape to the top flaps of cartons, ensuring secure closure from above, while bottom head machines seal the bottom flaps, providing stability during transport. Both types enhance efficiency and ensure product safety during storage and shipment, catering to different operational needs in the packaging industry. Their combined use optimizes the sealing process.</p></p>
