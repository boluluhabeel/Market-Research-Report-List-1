<p><p>The wood briquettes market comprises three primary types: solid cylindrical, RUF, and Pini-Kay. Solid cylindrical briquettes are uniform in shape, ideal for efficient combustion. RUF type briquettes are rectangular, offering high density and energy efficiency, suitable for industrial applications. Pini-Kay briquettes feature a distinctive shape with a hole in the center, promoting better airflow and combustion. Each type serves diverse market needs, driven by factors such as energy content, ease of handling, and combustion characteristics.</p></p>
