<p><strong>The PV(Photovoltaic) Tracker Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis Trackers</li><li>Dual Axis Trackers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photovoltaic (PV) tracker systems are devices that orient solar panels toward the sun to maximize energy capture. Single-axis trackers rotate along one axis, usually north to south, optimizing solar exposure throughout the day. They are cost-effective and widely used in large-scale solar farms. Dual-axis trackers adjust both vertically and horizontally, allowing for optimal orientation throughout the year, thus increasing energy yield. While more expensive, they provide higher efficiency and are suitable for locations with variable solar angles.</p></p>
