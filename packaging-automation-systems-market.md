<p><p>Packaging automation systems can be categorized into two primary types: liquid packaging automation systems and solid packaging automation systems. Liquid packaging systems focus on efficiently handling and packaging liquids, such as beverages and chemicals, using technologies like filling machines and capping systems. In contrast, solid packaging automation systems cater to products like granules, powders, and bulk items, employing methods such as bagging, carton filling, and wrapping machines. Both types enhance productivity, reduce labor costs, and ensure product consistency.</p></p>
