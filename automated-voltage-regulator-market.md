<p><strong>The Automated Voltage Regulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Regulator</li><li>Shunt Voltage Regulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Voltage Regulator market is primarily divided into two main types: Linear Regulators and Shunt Voltage Regulators. Linear Regulators provide a stable output voltage by dissipating excess voltage as heat, offering low noise and simplicity, often used in low-power applications. In contrast, Shunt Voltage Regulators maintain voltage by diverting excess current, ensuring regulation under varying loads. Each type caters to specific applications, influencing the market dynamics based on efficiency, cost, and performance requirements in diverse electronic systems.</p></p>
