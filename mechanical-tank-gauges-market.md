<p><p>Mechanical tank gauges are crucial for measuring fluid levels in various applications. The tubular type uses a graduated tube filled with the liquid to indicate levels. Reflection type gauges utilize light reflection off the fluid surface for measurement. Transparent type gauges have a clear viewing section, allowing direct visual observation of liquid levels. Bicolor type features dual colors to highlight different fluid levels clearly. Other types include specialized gauges designed for specific industries or unique tank configurations, enhancing measurement accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
