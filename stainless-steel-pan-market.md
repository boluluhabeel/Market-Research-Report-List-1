<p><p>The stainless steel pan market is divided into three main types based on composition: 200 series, 300 series, and 400 series stainless steel. The 200 series is cost-effective and offers decent corrosion resistance, making it suitable for everyday cooking. The 300 series, known for its high corrosion resistance and durability, is preferred for professional kitchens. The 400 series, with lower nickel content, is magnetic and less expensive, making it ideal for budget-friendly options but may be less resistant to rust and staining.</p></p>
