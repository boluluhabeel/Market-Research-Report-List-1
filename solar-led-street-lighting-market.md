<p><p>The Solar LED Street Lighting market primarily features two types of solar panels: Polycrystalline and Monocrystalline. Polycrystalline panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a cost-effective option with moderate efficiency. In contrast, Monocrystalline panels are composed of single silicon crystals, providing higher efficiency and better space utilization, albeit at a higher cost. Both types are used extensively in street lighting solutions, influencing factors like installation costs, energy production, and overall performance in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
