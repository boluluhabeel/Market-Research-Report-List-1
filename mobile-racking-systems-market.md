<p><p>Mobile racking systems are categorized into three types based on their control mechanisms. Manual control racking requires operators to physically move storage units, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Remote control racking allows users to operate the system from a distance, enhancing convenience while maintaining some manual intervention. Automatic control racking systems utilize advanced technology for fully automated movement and management of stored items, maximizing efficiency and space utilization, making them ideal for large-scale operations and high-density storage environments.</p></p>
