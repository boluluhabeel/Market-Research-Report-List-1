<p><p>The delivery drones market is primarily categorized into rotary-wing and fixed-wing types. Rotary-wing drones, often known as multi-rotors, excel in vertical takeoff and landing, providing agility and precision, making them ideal for urban deliveries. In contrast, fixed-wing drones are generally more efficient for long-distance travel, capable of covering larger areas at higher speeds. Each type serves distinct delivery needs, with rotary-wing drones focusing on short-range, high-frequency urban deliveries, while fixed-wing drones target longer-range logistics and rural applications.</p></p>
