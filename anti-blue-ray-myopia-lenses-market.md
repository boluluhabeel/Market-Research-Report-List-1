<p><ul><li>Spherical</li><li>Aspheric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-blue ray myopia lenses market features two primary types: spherical and aspheric lenses. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature across their surface, providing a uniform focal point, which aids in correcting myopia effectively. Aspheric lenses, on the other hand, have a varying curvature that reduces distortion and enhances visual clarity, particularly at the periphery. This design allows for slimmer profiles and lighter frames, catering to a demand for improved aesthetics and comfort while still protecting against harmful blue light exposure.</p></p>
